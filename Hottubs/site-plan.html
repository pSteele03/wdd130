Overview

For many students like myself, it is not uncommon to want to relax every so often. And because of this they pick their dorms/apartments based on one simple amenity, hot tubs... Due to the lack of information many students make the wrong choice and are stuck with lackluster hot tubs until their leases end. The goal of my website is to gather all the information up front about many of the local hot tubs so students can make the right choice and not suffer the same fate as many others new to the Rexburg area. In order to gather this information I will visit the hot tubs nearby and rate them on many things such as, temperature, cleanliness, vibes, and area of origin.

To inform students of good hot tub choices

Audience

All students looking for a bit more information before signing a lease
