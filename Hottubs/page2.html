FAQ

Why should I get a hot tub?

Hot tubs are a fantastic purchase for many groups of people, they are great for anyone who seeks relaxation weather it be after a long workout, or just a long life. Theyre also great for anyone who seeks to have an activity that the whole family can enjoy!

Whats the average cost to run a hot tub monthly?

While you might think that hot tub upkeep is a lot, it really is quite the opposite. The average monthly upkeep for a hot tub can be as little as $20 a month. Obviously this price can go a little bit higher around $75 if you have a really expensive hot tub and only buy name brand chemicals.

Whats a good middleground price for a hot tub?

Hot tub prices varie and a middlegroud price can be based on how nice of a hot tub it is that you want. While yes you can get a cheap one for around $200, you may want to spend a bit more. A great middleground for a decent hot tub normally sits around $1500.

Whats the best place to find a hot tub?

With todays advances in online shopping the answer is quite simple... Amazon!! Amazon has multiple options for hot tubs weather it be a cheap inflatable one or full size ones with excilent build qualities!
