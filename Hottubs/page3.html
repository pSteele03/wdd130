Top 5

#1 INTEX 28483E SimpleSpa

The INTEX 28483E SimpleSpa is one of the most popular hot tubs for a budget price on Amazon currently. Sitting at a price around $450 this hot tub comes packed with many features. The INTEX 28483E SimpleSpa is an inflatable hot tub that sits around a comfortable 104 degrees, has a built in bubble massage system and can comfortably fit 4 hot tubbers with ease.

#2 Bestway Coleman Palm Springs

The Bestway Coleman Palm Springs hot tub is another popular hot tub for those on a budget, sitting around $600-$650. Just like the INTEX SimpleSpa the temperature sits around a comfortable 104 degrees. The Bestway Coleman Palm Springs hot tub can seat up to six people, and has an increased three layer durable walls to ensure that the hot tub doesnt get damaged. The Bestway Coleman Palm Springs hot also has an increased number of air jets built in, a whopping 140 seperate jets along the inside.

#3 Jersey 6

The Jersey 6 is an expensive hot tub but that doesnt come without a better quality. The Jersey 6 sits around $4500. Made out of high quality metal, wood, and, plastic the Jersey 6 has a high build quality over other inflatable hot tubs yet lacks in portability. The Jersey 6 has 46 massaging jets built into the walls and can easily seat 8 people without issue.

#4 INTEX 28431EP

The INTEX 28431EP is another inflatabale hot tub. Similar to the INTEX 28483E SimpleSpa when it comes to build quality. The only thing that INTEX 28431EP has over its superior brother the INTEX 28483E is that it can fit two extra people.

#5 Bestway SaluSpa Hawaii

The Bestway SaluSpa Hawaii is the last on the list. It is another budget hot tub but the least chosen among the top 5. The Bestway SaluSpa Hawaii sits around $500. It is an inflatable and has 140 air jets built in. One downside it has over the other hot tubs on the list is that it can only seat 4 people.
